FAQ

Tap to toggle
Who can participate?

Students from any college (with valid ID). Some events allow open category entries.

Is there an entry fee?

Most events are free. Premium events (HackSprint, Beat Street) require a fest pass.

Will certificates be provided?

Yes—digital certificates for winners and participants where applicable.
